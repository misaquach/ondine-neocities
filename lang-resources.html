Language resources

To pirate textbooks, I recommend against Anna’s Archive, as it’s unethical to pirate from rich college textbook publishers.

General

Wiktionary
A publicly edited international dictionary providing words and phrases’ etymologies, translations, inflections, and meanings. I use it all the time

Learning Chocolate
Basic words in various languages.

Duolingo Classroom
My retired teacher’s Duolingo class. Gives you infinite hearts, but removes all your friends.

Anki
A flashcard app for P.C. with spaced repetition (cards’ review frequency depends on how well you know them.)

Latin

Latin Core List
A list of the most common words used in Latin.

Latin P.D.F. Notes
Grammar lessons

Latin Declensions
Allows the practice of noun declenions.

LatinTutorial
Extensive discussions of grammar and poetry.

Neo-Latin Lexicon
What it says on the tin. The downside is, it’s pretty laggy.

Immersion

Vicipaedia
Publicly edited Latin articles -- grammatical quality varies, but there’s a lot of vocabulary.

LatinTutorial: The Aeneid
Walkthrough of Virgil’s famed poem

Cupiditates de Elysii
This is definitely not pirated L.L.P.S.I.; I cannot be held liable for anything this link contains.

Latinium
Articles and audio -- some English, some Latin

The Latin Library
Latin reading material. Also has grammar sheets here

Georgicon
An English-Latin text of Virgil’s poem

Bolchazy-Carducci
A shop of Latin texts and workbooks.

Russian

OpenRussian
Word lists for various levels.

Be Fluent in Russian
Discussions of vocabulary, grammar, and culture.

Russian Through Propaganda / Through Poetry and Paintings, Mark Pettus
A series decorated with Soviet posters and Imperial paintings. Likely the best textbook series for self-studiers.

Troika: A Communicative Approach to Russian Language, Life, and Culture, Marita Nummikoski
More slow-paced than Pettus’ books, with more everyday vocabulary and knowledge

Immersion

Russian Film Hub
Subtitled Russian and Soviet films.

По-русски
Short stories for beginners in Russian.

Poetry Reader for Russian Learners
Poems of famous Russian authors with translated vocabulary.

РуСтих
Extensive poetry collection, but without translations

Ancient Greek

Lexis Rex
Basic vocabulary lists.

Nifty Greek Handouts
P.D.F. files on grammar

From Alpha to Omega, Anne Groton
Contains vocabulary, excerpts, grammar lessons, and exercises; pretty comprehensive.

Greek to GSCE, Mark Taylor
Originally designed for British schools, but good for self-studying. Slower paced than Groton’s book.

Immersion

Argos
A fully Ancient Greek podcast.

AncientGreek
Numerous Greek audiobooks.
